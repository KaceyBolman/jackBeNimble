jackBeNimble

bitcoin order entry made simple
Usage
  • Use hotkeys to place limit and market orders.
  • Place an order at, below, or above the current best bid or offer.
  • Use alt keys to place limit orders at set intervals above or below market
  • Increase and decrease lot sizes.
  • Custom config hotkeys.
