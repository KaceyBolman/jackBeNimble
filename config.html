Keybindings

TOGGLE_LOTSIZE_UP: 220, TOGGLE_LOTSIZE_DOWN: 222, TOGGLE_INCR_UP: 189, TOGGLE_INCR_DOWN: 187, INCR_BID: 68, INCR_OFFER: 75, OFFER_WITH_BEST_ASK: 76, BID_WITH_BEST_BID: 83, OFFER_ABOVE_BEST: 186, BID_BELOW_BEST: 65, MARKET_BUY: 71, MARKET_SELL: 72
Usage
  • Use hotkeys to place limit and market orders.
  • Place an order at, below, or above the current best bid or offer.
  • Use alt keys to place limit orders at set intervals above or below market
  • Increase and decrease lot sizes.
  • Custom config hotkeys.
